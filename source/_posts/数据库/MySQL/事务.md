## 事务有关的表

- information_schema.innodb_trx–当前运行的所有事务，
- information_schema.innodb_locks–当前出现的锁
- information_schema.innodb_lock_waits–锁等待的对应关系

| Field                      | Type                | Null | Key | Default             | Extra                   |
| -------------------------- | ------------------- | ---- | --- | ------------------- | ----------------------- |
